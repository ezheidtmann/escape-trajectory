string RestrictedThree::getOptionString ( )
inlinevirtual

Additional command-line options specific to this problem.

Unless otherwise noted, every option takes as an argument a single floating-point value.

  • -f: earth mass fraction
  • -R: launch radius
  • -V: launch speed
  • -q: launch angle (q = theta)
  • -c: capture radius
  • -X: launch speed in the X direction (-P is ignored). This or -Y must appear after -R if specified.
  • -Y: launch speed in the Y direction (-P is ignored). This or -X must appear after -R if specified.
  • -M: magnification factor for velocity parameter
  • -S: minimum ("starting") velocity for component-wise velocity parameter
  • -P: treat launch speed (-V) as the "parameter" , rather than as the relative speed . This option does not take an argument.
  • -G: treate launch speed (-X or -Y) as pseudo-energy. This argument does not take an argument.
  • -E: enable recording pseudo-energy. This option does not take an argument.
  • -D: enable recording total distance covered
  • -W: enable recording "winding number", or total net radians (measured from the center of mass) covered. Large winding numbers correlate with loops; small numbers mean straight shots or switchbacks.
