void RestrictedThree::recordData ( realtype  t,
N_Vector  z 
)
virtual

Record data to standard output.

Each line consists of 3 to 8 columns separated by tab characters. The first line begins with a "#" character and indicates the meaning of each of the columns:

  • t: time, where is one complete revolution of the two-body system (one "year")
  • x: x-coordinate, relative to the center of mass
  • y: y-coordinate, relative to the center of mass
  • e ( ): relative change in the pseudo-energy of the system
  • d: total distance covered by the projectile over the integration period
  • wE: winding number, with center of rotation at the earth
  • wS: winding number, with center of rotation at the sun
  • wC: winding number, with center of rotation at the center of mass.
Parameters
tTime value at this data point
zValue of the data vector at this point
