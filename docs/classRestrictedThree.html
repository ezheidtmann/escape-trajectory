Detailed Description

Implements the restricted three-body problem.

In this problem, there are two bodies of comparable mass orbiting their common center of mass. Due to carefully chosen initial conditions, we can assume that they orbit the center of mass in concentric circles. We call these two bodies Earth and Sun, but the mass ratio is not normally as extreme as in the actual solar system.
